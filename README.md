# LAPATA

## AI-Powered Missing Person Reconnect Platform

---

# 🌍 Vision

**Lapata** is a modern community-powered platform designed to help reconnect missing children, elderly people, mentally unstable individuals, and untraceable persons with their families.

The platform uses:

* memory-based searching,
* AI-assisted matching,
* locality-based identification,
* and public community support

to make finding lost people faster, easier, and more human-centered.

---

# 💡 Problem Statement

Most missing people:

* do not remember phone numbers,
* cannot recall exact addresses,
* may not carry IDs,
* and often feel confused or unsafe.

However, they usually remember:

* mother or father names,
* sibling names,
* school names,
* villages,
* colonies,
* landmarks,
* or nearby places.

Current systems rely heavily on:

* police reports,
* IDs,
* phone numbers,
* or manual searching.

There is no fast, intelligent, public-friendly platform focused on **human memory fragment search**.

---

# 🚀 Solution

Lapata provides a public searchable platform where:

✅ Families can raise missing person reports
✅ Nearby helpers can search using remembered details
✅ AI chatbot assists with smart matching
✅ Public users can help reconnect families
✅ Active cases remain public only until resolved

Once the missing person is found:

* the case is automatically removed from public visibility,
* archived privately,
* and marked as resolved.

---

# 🧠 Core Innovation

## Human Memory Fragment Search

Instead of relying only on:

* mobile numbers,
* IDs,
* exact names,

Lapata allows searching using:

* mother name,
* father name,
* sibling names,
* locality,
* village,
* landmarks,
* schools,
* partial information.

Example:

> “A child says his mother name is Sunita and he lives near Rohini.”

The system intelligently searches and suggests matching families.

---

# 🤖 AI-Powered Features

## AI Smart Search Chatbot

Users can describe the found person naturally.

Example:

* “Old man says his son name Amit.”
* “Child from Bihar near railway station.”

The AI:

* extracts meaningful information,
* searches the database,
* ranks probable matches,
* and helps reconnect families faster.

# 🔍 Main Features

## Public Search System

* No login required
* Search by names, locality, village, school, landmarks
* Smart filters
* Fast search experience

---

## Missing Person Reporting

Authenticated family members can:

* create reports,
* upload photos,
* add details,
* manage active cases,
* mark cases as found.

---

## Found Someone Workflow

Helpers can:

* upload current images,
* share location,
* contact families,
* and assist in reunification.

---

## Case Resolution System

When a person is found:

* public visibility is removed,
* search indexing is removed,
* and the case is archived privately.

---

# 🔐 Privacy & Safety

Lapata is designed with privacy-first principles.

The platform NEVER publicly exposes:

* exact home addresses,
* Aadhaar details,
* sensitive documents,
* private identity information.

Only safe searchable information is displayed publicly.
